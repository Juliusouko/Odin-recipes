Pizza Recipe

Homemade pizza is a delicious and fun meal to make for the whole family.
This recipe is easy to make and tastes great!
You can make it with store-bought dough or make your own from scratch.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Olive oil
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Vegetables

Steps to follow:

  1. Combine flour, yeast, sugar, and salt in a bowl
  2. Add water and olive oil and mix until a dough forms
  3. Knead the dough for 5-10 minutes until smooth
  4. Let the dough rise for 1-2 hours
  5. Roll out the dough and top with tomato sauce, cheese, and toppings
  6. Bake at 450Â°F for 15-20 minutes
